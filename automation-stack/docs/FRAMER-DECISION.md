# Framer Decision

## Use Framer if:
- the public site becomes a design-first marketing showroom
- the live stack needs faster visual iteration than code-first site changes
- app logic stays elsewhere

## Do not use Framer if:
- it becomes another competing production root
- it fragments the stack further
- it delays real onboarding and monetization work

## Best use
Framer = public marketing surface only.
